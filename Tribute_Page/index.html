Time line of Ada Lovelace

  • 1815
    • Born.

  • 1816
    • Parents Separate.

  • 1824
    • Lord Byron, Ada's father dies in a war.

  • 1828
    • Ada designs a flying.

  • 1829
    • Ada gets measles and is unable to leave bed, this last 3 years.

  • 1832
    • Recovers from measles

  • 1833
    • Ada meets Charles Babbage, she is inspired by his words.

  • 1835
    • Ada weds William King.

  • 1836
    • Ada's first child is born with Byron's name.

  • 1837
    • Ada's second child is born with Anne Isabella's name.

  • 1838
    • Ada becomes Countess of Lovelace.

  • 1839
    • Ada's third child is born with Ralph Gordon's name.

  • 1846
    • Ada's translation and Notes on the analytical engine are published. This is her life's achievement, inside is what is considered to by the worlds first computer program.

  • 1852
    • Ada Lovelace died at the age of thirty-six from uterine cancer. She was buried, at her request, next to her father at the Church of St. Mary Magdalene in Hucknall, Nottingham.
